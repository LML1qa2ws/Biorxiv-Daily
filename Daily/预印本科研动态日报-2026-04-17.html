01
Gut microbiome composition and predicted functions relate to growth and behavior in a Japanese preschool cohort
日本学龄前队列研究：肠道菌群组成及预测功能与生长发育及行为的相关性
bioRxiv Ichikawa, S., Shimura, A. et al. 通讯：Yasuhito Shimada 2026-04-16
亮点：日本学龄前儿童横断面研究揭示肠道菌群组成与行为发育的关联模式，为理解菌群-肠-脑轴在神经发育中的作用提供社区队列证据。
摘要
Early childhood is a period of rapid brain maturation and gut microbiome assembly, when emerging behavioral difficulties can shape later mental health and learning trajectories. Microbiota-gut-brain communication has been implicated in neurodevelopment through microbial metabolites and immune signaling.
主要发现
  • 内部化行为域与高炎症潜在特征和核苷酸生物合成升高相关
  • 躯体主诉和退缩行为与呼吸/发酵活性降低相关
  • 睡眠相关困难显示最广泛的预测功能足迹
  • 外化域与细胞膜和碳水化合物重塑通路相关
  • 年龄/身高/体重追踪肠道菌群典型成熟轨迹
方法横断面社区队列 + 16S rRNA测序 + PICRUSt2功能预测 + 行为评估量表
肠道菌群免疫治疗神经科学

Fiber Formulation-Dependent Modulation of Gut Microbial Metabolism in Parkinson's Disease
配方纤维依赖性调节帕金森病患者肠道微生物代谢
bioRxiv Todd, O. A., Vu, L. D. et al. 通讯：Olivia Adele Todd 2026-04-14
亮点：SIFR技术平台证实配方纤维复杂度决定微生物代谢参与度，多纤维组合使短链脂肪酸产量翻倍，为帕金森病肠道干预提供精准配方依据。
摘要
Parkinsons disease (PD) is associated with altered gut-brain signaling, including microbial dysbiosis, intestinal inflammation, and reduced short-chain fatty acid (SCFA) production.
主要发现
  • 多纤维组合使SCFA产量增加约2倍(p=0.001)
  • 微生物生物量增加约1.5倍(p=0.0007)
  • 富集纤维响应分类群，协调代谢物转变
方法SIFR体外肠道发酵平台 + SCFA定量 + 微生物组16S分析 + 代谢组学
肠道菌群帕金森病神经科学微生物代谢
05
Cell fusion reprograms tumor cells and promotes RUNX1-mediated invasion and dissemination in colorectal cancer
细胞融合重编程肿瘤细胞并促进RUNX1介导的结直肠癌侵袭与远处播散
bioRxiv Anderson, A. N., Queitsch, K. et al. 通讯：Melissa H. Wong 2026-04-15
亮点：首次揭示肿瘤-巨噬细胞融合杂交细胞中RUNX1作为转移驱动核心调控因子，循环杂交细胞(CHCs)可作为非侵入性肿瘤进展生物标志物。
摘要
Metastasis remains the primary cause of cancer-related morbidity and mortality, despite significant advances in targeted therapies. Although metastatic dissemination requires tumor cells to escape the primary lesion and colonize distant organs, the mechanisms by which primary tumor cells gain metastatic competence remain poorly understood.
主要发现
  • RUNX1是杂交细胞运动和侵袭的核心调控因子
  • RUNX1敲低抑制蛋白酶表达、趋化活性和ECM侵袭
  • RUNX1药理抑制减少杂交肿瘤生长和外周血播散
  • 循环杂交细胞(CHCs)富集迁移和EMT表型，可作为液体活检标志物
方法scRNA-seq + cyCIF + 体外杂交细胞模型 + 体内药理抑制

EML4-ALK Fusion Rewires Transcriptomic, miRNA, and CAF-Associated Programs in Non-Small Cell Lung Cancer
EML4-ALK融合重塑非小细胞肺癌转录组、miRNA及CAF相关程序
bioRxiv Mishra, D., Agrawal, S. et al. 通讯：Rajeev Mishra 2026-04-16
亮点：整合mRNA-miRNA谱与肿瘤免疫微环境，首次系统解析EML4-ALK融合阳性NSCLC的转录后调控网络和CAF相关ECM程序重塑，为精准治疗提供超越激酶抑制的广谱视角。
摘要
This study establishes an integrative framework that combines paired mRNA/miRNA profiling with immune microenvironmental features to clarify how EML4-ALK fusions shape transcriptomic and post-transcriptional networks in Non-small cell lung cancer (NSCLC).
主要发现
  • 融合阳性NSCLC代谢/蛋白稳态通路激活，免疫基质通讯减弱
  • miR-3065调控枢纽特异上调，靶向ECM/黏附基因
  • 融合阳性肿瘤CAF浸润低于融合阴性，基因-CAF关联模式不同
方法配对mRNA-seq + miRNA-seq + 肿瘤免疫微环境分析 + CAF浸润评估

JAK-STAT Pathway Heterogeneity Governs Immunotherapy Response in Breast Cancer
JAK-STAT通路异质性调控乳腺癌免疫治疗疗效
bioRxiv Zhou, J., Zhang, H. et al. 通讯：Fu Peng 2026-02-05
亮点：JAK-STAT通路在不同细胞类型中展现功能异质性——在肿瘤细胞促癌、在T细胞增强抗肿瘤免疫，STAT4作为双面生物标志物可预测TNBC患者PD-1抑制剂疗效。
摘要
The JAK-STAT pathway (JSP) is a well-known oncogenic cascade; however, recent clinical trials have detected JSP upregulation in breast cancer following anti-PD1 immunotherapy.
主要发现
  • JAK-STAT通路在肿瘤细胞、T细胞和旁癌细胞中展现动态异质性
  • STAT4双面调控：肿瘤细胞促癌，T细胞增强抗肿瘤功能
  • 高JAK-STAT活性与TNBC患者PD-1抑制剂疗效改善相关
方法JAK-STAT通路分析 + 多套单细胞数据整合 + 免疫治疗数据集验证

Nanoparticle-delivered resiquimod induces brain tumor regression in medulloblastoma and diffuse midline glioma models by interrupting paracrine growth support and activating myeloid immune signaling and phagocytosis
纳米颗粒递送Resiquimod通过中断旁分泌生长支持并激活髓系免疫信号与吞噬作用诱导髓母细胞瘤和弥漫性中线胶质瘤消退
bioRxiv McSwain, L. F., Kim, K. et al. 通讯：Timothy R Gershon 2026-04-09
亮点：ResiPOx纳米颗粒突破血脑屏障，系统性给药激活小胶质/巨噬细胞驱动的抗肿瘤免疫，在MB和DMG临床前模型中延长无进展生存期，非人灵长类验证安全性。
摘要
We studied the effect of stimulating innate immune function in tumor-associated myeloid cells (TAMs) in medulloblastoma (MB) and diffuse midline glioma (DMG), using a polyoxazoline nanoparticle formulation of the TLR7/8 agonist resiquimod (ResiPOx).
主要发现
  • ResiPOx配方改善血脑屏障穿透和抗肿瘤疗效
  • ResiPOx延长MB和DMG小鼠无进展生存期
  • TAM重编程：IFNβ/外源性凋亡通路激活，抗原呈递和T细胞激活上调
  • 非人灵长类安全验证，脑转录响应与小鼠DMG/MB模型相似
方法聚[恶]唑啉纳米颗粒 + 临床前MB/DMG内源性模型 + 非人灵长类PK/PD

09
Midkine Dependency Defines a Therapeutically Actionable Vulnerability in Medulloblastoma
Midkine依赖性定义髓母细胞瘤中可靶向的治疗脆弱点
bioRxiv Jayamohan, S., Venkata, P. P. et al. 通讯：Gangadhara R Sareddy 2026-04-14
亮点：MDK通过多受体(NCL/LRP1/SDC2)网络整合癌基因信号、核糖体生物合成和先天免疫，CRISPR敲除和抗体中和均显著抑制MB生长，揭示难治性儿童脑瘤全新治疗靶点。
摘要
Medulloblastoma (MB), the most common malignant pediatric brain tumor, remains associated with high recurrence rates and substantial treatment-related morbidity despite aggressive multimodal therapy. Here, we identify midkine (MDK), a secreted growth factor/cytokine, as a previously unrecognized central regulator of MB growth and survival.
主要发现
  • MDK通过多受体(NCL/LRP1/SDC2)维持ERK1/2和Akt-mTOR-S6信号
  • MDK沉默抑制核糖体生物合成、蛋白质翻译和MYC驱动
  • MDK缺失触发IFNγ-cGAS-STING通路激活，连接翻译应激与先天免疫
  • CRISPR敲除MDK显著减少原位移植瘤生长并延长生存期
方法CRISPR敲除 + 单细胞RNA-seq + 磷酸化激酶谱 + 体内原位移植模型

Signal-to-noise evaluation of dynamic versus static ¹⁸F-FDG-PET in focal epilepsy via Bayesian regional estimated signal quality analysis
基于贝叶斯区域信号质量分析的动态与静态¹⁸F-FDG-PET在局灶性癫痫中的信噪比评估
medRxiv Quigg, M., Chernyavskiy, P. et al. 通讯：Mark Quigg 2026-04-14
亮点：BRESQ贝叶斯区域信号质量分析客观量化iD-PET信噪比优于sPET，支持动态PET作为癫痫手术评估非侵入性工具，为难治性癫痫精准定位提供新方法。
摘要
Background and Purpose: 2-[18F] fluoro-2-deoxy-D-glucose positron emission tomography (static PET) has mixed specificity and sensitivity in targeting epileptic zones in the noninvasive stage of epilepsy surgery evaluations. We compared the signal quality of static PET compared to a method of interictal dynamic PET (iD-PET).
主要发现
  • iD-PET在36个ROI中29个(80%)的SNR优于sPET，概率>95%占8个ROI
  • SNR优势最大区域：颞内侧(左右)、枕外侧(左右)、左额叶基底
  • BRESQ方法可扩展并可泛化至不同脑mapping模态
方法贝叶斯区域估计信号质量分析(BRESQ) + 动态PET vs静态PET对比

MICRON：学习与预后相关的空间免疫微环境表征
bioRxiv Chen, C.-J., George, B. et al. 通讯：Natalie Stanley 2026-04-16

Spatial imaging proteomics modalities, such as imaging mass cytometry, enable comprehensive identification of immune microenvironments driving disease outcomes.
